Purpose

How we are fighting inequalities

The LGBT+ population in Zimbabwe faces difficulty accessing quality medicine due to discrimination. Through the multitude of possibilities offered by the digital era, and the generosity of our volunteers and donors, today we can provide online medical consultations. We unite the desire to offer assistance to those who need support. In this way, we can all contribute to a more equal Southern Africa.

Making health care possible

Your help is our biggest ally

We are transforming the way LGBT+ Zimbabweans can access healthcare, through quality consultations and medicines. Lives are being saved thanks to our network, and there are many ways to contribute. You can - right now - change the lives of countless inhabitants of a country that needs your help.
And making this happen is easier than it looks. You can help through your time. Through financial support, and, believe us, the smallest dollar still counts. And even through your network, expanding the reach of our community!
We're very grateful you're here. Your presence helps us build a safer Southern Africa for the LGBT+ population of Zimbabwe.

Donations

Donating is an essential way to keep the program going. Contributing financially to Doctors for Zimbabwe directly impacts democratic access to healthcare in that country. Our costs involve the website domain, in addition to publicity campaigns, to expand the network of professionals involved. We have a program for purchasing medicines, through proof of income.
